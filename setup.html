Username*

Your username is a public information for people to recognize you when Sprint+ will work with servers. It also allows Sprint+ to send you custom messages. A username is currently one-use only.
You will be able to change it later, but once Sprint+ servers will be released, you will receive a custom key that will be your identifier.

Learn moreabout what's coming regarding usernames.

E-Mail adress*

Your email adress is at the moment useless. However, it will be useful for upcoming updates. You will be able to change it later.

Learn moreabout what's coming regarding emails.

Password*

Enter a secure password to enforce your account. You won't be able to find your password with your email adress for the moment. You will still be able to modify it. Write it somewhere, or keep it in mind.
